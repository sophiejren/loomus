A note from Sophie

LOOMUS began with a question — what becomes of us when the systems we build start to think? To answer it, perhaps we have to go further back — to the origins of cognition itself, and to what minds were for in the first place.

If you've read this far, thank you for being here. This is less a publication than an inquiry — and we are not so much building as discovering, together.

— Sophie · Founder, LOOMUS
About LOOMUS

A global community for the AI era

LOOMUS is a global community for the AI era — publishing books, hosting gatherings, and connecting the people building and thinking about artificial intelligence. We work in six languages, across three continents.

It has grown into a meeting point for the people defining this era — scientists and decision-makers from frontier labs like Anthropic and OpenAI, AI founders, investors from firms like a16z, professors from Stanford and MIT, and researchers across disciplines.

Build the AI era — keep it human, and think in decades.

Global by design. The "loom" in our name says what we set out to do: to weave Silicon Valley, Europe and Asia — three homes of innovation — together like threads. Each year LOOMUS hosts and co-curates flagship summits across San Francisco, Paris, London and beyond, alongside media partners like Forbes and Hurun, so good ideas reach the world and talent moves freely across borders.

Human at heart. More than connecting resources, LOOMUS cares about the intention behind the connection. We continue to support academic gatherings, tech-for-good projects and quiet conversations — Stanford's Demo Day, the Robot Marathon, salons with researchers and writers — because we believe the most advanced innovation should, in the end, return warmly to people themselves.

Built for the long view. LOOMUS counts in decades, not quarters. The books we publish, the gatherings we host, and the connections that form between them — the founder in Berlin who meets the investor in Shanghai because they both read LOOMUS first — are all chapters in a longer project: a home for learning and collaboration in the AI era.

— Sophie Ren, Founder

Find the lane that's yours

LOOMUS is for builders, thinkers, writers and artists with a sense of civilization — people making ambitious things without losing their humanity along the way. Six lanes in.

01

Builders of Intelligence

For founders, researchers, technologists and operators building the infrastructure of the future.

"We're interested in people building ambitious systems without losing their humanity in the process."

02

Researchers

For AI researchers, scientists, postdocs, PhDs and applied research engineers working on the technical and scientific frontier of intelligence.

"We talk to the people building the frontier — and right now we're funding interviews for an ongoing research project."

03

Writers & Cultural Memory

For essayists, critics, storytellers, interviewers and documentarians — people who preserve the emotional memory of an era.

"We believe communities without writers lose their memory."

04

Artists of Intelligence

For artists, designers, musicians, architects, creative coders, filmmakers and aesthetic technologists exploring new forms of intelligence.

"The future will not be shaped by engineers alone — but also by the people designing how intelligence feels."

Humanists of the Machine Age

For anthropologists, philosophers, cognitive scientists, historians, writers and thinkers exploring what intelligence means for humans.
