The Bay Area Room Map — 30 AI events, sorted by what you actually leave with.

By Sophie Ren · Founder, LOOMUS · May 30, 2026 Subscribe weekly → 14 min · 1 chart · 1 matrix

San Francisco has too many AI events, and every roundup post is the same: 50 things to do, ranked by date. This isn't one of those. Below is a 2D map of every room worth being in, plotted by intimacy and cadence — and then a matrix that answers the only question that matters: what do I leave with?

Weekly A new issue lands every Saturday morning — same lens, fresh rooms. See all issues →

LOOMUS curates this list every Saturday. Submissions: events@loomus.ai. The live calendar is at loomus.ai/events.

§ 01 The Room Map

Every dot is one event. Vertical axis = intimacy (how many people in the room). Horizontal axis = cadence (how often it happens). Five archetype zones color-coded.

Reading the map: the clusters tell the story. Weekly intimate rooms dominate the lower-left — labs and lobbies. Annual flagship halls stack the upper-right. The interesting empty space is the middle: mid-sized monthly events with curatorial seriousness — the gap LOOMUS spends most of its programming budget on.

Reading the matrix: halls are best for customer + connection, but worst for finding a co-founder. Salons are the only room that produces co-founders consistently. Workshops are the only room where you'll meet someone willing to ship a prototype with you on Saturday. Labs are where the next year's papers get drafted. Lobbies are how you stay in the city's rhythm without burning a week. Stack one of each into your month and the math works out.

The right room beats the right idea. The right matrix beats the right room.

Archetype 01 · 1000+ · Annual
Marquee Hall

The flagship gatherings — the ones with multi-track programming and announcements that move markets. Worth the price of admission for the hallway alone, not the keynote.

Jun 15–182026

Databricks Data + AI Summit

Moscone, SF · $1,395–$1,895 · 4 days Customer

The single largest data and ML conference of the year. Heavy on data engineering, governance, and the shift from analytics to agentic workflows. If you build data infrastructure, this is unmissable.

Jun 29–Jul 22026

AI Engineer World's Fair

Marines' Memorial, SF · $999–$1,699 Hire

The world's largest technical conference dedicated to building, deploying, and scaling AI systems. Last year's edition produced more open-source library launches than any other AI event of the year.

Sep 29–Oct 12026

The AI Conference 2026

Pier 48, SF · 3 days · Single track Idea

Single-track, founder-heavy gathering with views the rest of the conferences can't compete with. Smaller than World's Fair but conversations land harder.

Nov 122026

Cerebral Valley AI Summit

SF · Invite-only · One day Connection

The hardest ticket of the year. The AI world's startup leaders in one room. Recent lineups include Anthropic's chief product officer (former Instagram founder) and the co-founder of xAI.

Q42026

Anthropic · Code with Claude (follow-up)

SF + livestream · Free Paper

Anthropic's developer conference returns after the May SF/London/Tokyo trilogy. Full-day workshops, 1:1 office hours with engineers, and announcements that shift the agent-builder roadmap.

Sep2026

SaaStr Annual

San Mateo Event Center · $749–$1,299 · 10,000+ attendees Customer

The world's largest community gathering for B2B and AI leaders. The 2026 edition centers Deploy '26, the first dedicated summit for agentic AI in production.

Jun 52026

AI-tonomy Summit: Models & Agents LOOMUS host

Plug and Play, Silicon Valley · 20% off for LOOMUS readers Customer

A focused day on autonomous AI and the agent stack — foundation models to production-grade infrastructure. 20% off via SOPHIESFRIEND on the LOOMUS Events calendar.

Fall2026

AGI Summit & GenAI Summit 2026 LOOMUS flagship

San Francisco · Single-track Connection

The successor to GenAI Week SV and GenAI Summit Paris. Single-track day for researchers, frontier-model builders, and investors. Salon evening at a private venue.

Nov2026

Asia-Pacific AI+ Innovation Summit LOOMUS × Hurun

Hong Kong · 2 days · 9 sub-forums Connection

The flagship APAC AI event of the year. The first event formally under the LOOMUS name in Asia. Details on loomus.ai/events.

Jan2027

Silicon Valley Robot Marathon · SVRM/27 LOOMUS inaugural

Bay Area Peninsula · 21.1 km Idea

The Valley's first public half-marathon for autonomous machines — humanoid, quadruped, wheeled. Partner seats and team entries open through Q4 2026. Full site at loomus.ai/svrm-27.

Archetype 02 · 20–50 · Monthly
The Salon

The only room that produces co-founders consistently. Smaller, harder to get into, slower to pay off — and worth every minute of the application.

QuarterlyVol 1+

Founders Bay VIP Dinners

SF · Closed-door · ~25 per dinner Co-founder

Founders Bay is one of the largest AI-focused founder communities in SF. The quarterly VIPs are closed-door, tier-curated, and yield more co-investor introductions per evening than most conferences yield per week.

Jun 22026

AI Tinkerers · VIP Dinner: Harnessing Agents

SF · Invite-curated Hire

AI Tinkerers' founder-and-engineer dinner. June's theme: agent architectures — RAG, tool use, evals, observability. Real prototypes shown over wine.

Monthly2025–26

CRA-I · Jeffersonian Dinners

University Club of SF · 20 seats Paper

Founder-academic crossover dinners. Recent topic: "How AI will reshape computer systems by 2035." Past co-hosts include Dave Patterson (UC Berkeley/Google) and Jeff Dean (Google AI).

Monthly2026

AGI House · Founder Dinners

Hillsborough · Closed-door Co-founder

The original AI founder house, in a mansion in Hillsborough. Demo nights, monthly hackathons, dinners that run later than they should. Co-programmed with Berkeley AI Research, Stanford HAI, and frontier labs.

Monthly2026

South Park Commons · SPC Salons

SF · Members + 1 guest Idea

SPC's signature salon. Members bring one guest. Topics seeded a week in advance. The audience is mostly between projects — which is when the most interesting introductions happen.

Monthly2026

Founders Inc · Demo & Supper

Fort Mason · 30–40 founders Idea

Demo three things, eat dinner, argue about one of the demos for two hours. The Founders Inc team has spent three years tuning this format.

MonthlyVol 7+ LOOMUS host

LOOMUS · Founders Salon

SF & Palo Alto · Closed-door Co-founder

LOOMUS's invite-only salon series, paused since Vol 6 and reopening Q3 2026. Sophie hosts in person; agenda set 72 hours ahead by attendees themselves. Apply via events@loomus.ai.

WeeklyOngoing

Foresight Institute · AI Salon

SF Mission · Open · Free Paper

Foresight's weekly AI salon brings researchers and engineers into conversation with founders and writers. Heavier on alignment, philosophy, long-horizon questions than most rooms above. Free, public, RSVP-based.

Archetype 03 · 50–200 · Recurring
Workshop

Where co-founders meet over GitHub commits and prototypes ship in 48 hours. The places where careers get launched on a Saturday.

Jun2026

UC Berkeley · AI Hackathon

UC Berkeley · 2 days · Cash + tech prizes Hire

Berkeley's flagship student-and-builder hackathon. Workshops with frontier-lab engineers, judging panels with founders. Open to non-students.

QuarterlyOngoing

Llama Lounge × Snowflake Hackathon

Snowflake Campus, Menlo Park · Sponsored Paper

Llama Lounge's first-ever hosted hackathon at Snowflake's Menlo Park campus, focused on agentic AI. Backed by CrewAI, Composio, Skyfire, and Llama Lounge.

WeeklyTuesdays

Codex Community · SF Hackathon

Lower Haight · Tuesday evening · Free Hire

Tatiana's weekly hackathon series — drop in for 3 hours after work, ship a small prototype, leave with feedback from someone who's shipped a startup.

Monthly2026

AGI House · Saturday Build Day

Hillsborough · Selected builders Co-founder

The original residence-style hackathon. Twenty-five builders per Saturday, with frontier-lab employees as in-house mentors.

Monthly2026

Founders Inc · Pitch & Demo Night

Fort Mason · 5-minute slots Customer

Five-minute pitches in front of a live VC table. Tight, fast, brutally helpful. Some of the 2025 cohort's pitches led directly to seed checks.

Monthly2026

Hire My Friend · Pitch Night

Workato HQ, SF Hire

An inverted pitch night: founders pitch why someone should join them, not why an investor should fund them. The right format for the current hiring market.

MonthlyStanford GSB

Stanford · Founders Demo Day

Stanford Campus · Closed cohort Customer

Spring/fall demo days for Stanford-affiliated founders. Curated for early-stage product polish in front of an investor audience. LOOMUS has co-hosted past editions.

Monthly2026

Pier 70 · Builder Showcase

Dogpatch · Open to public Idea

A neighborhood-flavored monthly showcase for hardware, robotics, and embodied-AI demos that don't fit a normal demo day. The crossover between software AI and physical AI happens here first.

Archetype 04 · 10–30 · Weekly
The Lab

Where the next year's papers get drafted, defended, and argued over. Often the most rigorous conversations in the Bay Area happen here.

WeeklyWednesdays

Stanford HAI · Seminar Series

Stanford Campus · Public · Free Paper

HAI's weekly seminar — guest speakers from frontier labs, policy researchers, Stanford faculty. Recordings posted, but the in-room Q&A is the value.

WeeklyFridays

Berkeley CALL AI · Friday Lunch

UC Berkeley · Public · Free + bring lunch Paper

The Berkeley AI lab's weekly lunch with rotating speakers. Lower formality than Stanford HAI; higher density of working-paper conversations.

Monthly2026 LOOMUS co-host

Stanford × LOOMUS · AI × Education Roundtable

Stanford Campus · Closed · Half-day Paper

Half-day roundtable bringing together AI researchers, education leaders, and EdTech founders. The 2024 session yielded a published whitepaper. The 2026 session targets AI literacy and the role of curated knowledge.

AnnualApr–May

Stanford AI Index · Launch Event

Stanford Campus · Public Connection

The annual launch of the Stanford AI Index, the most-cited industry benchmark report. Includes a curated panel and Q&A with the authors.

Quarterly2026

UC Berkeley · BAIR Open House

UC Berkeley · Public · Free Hire

Berkeley AI Research's quarterly open house. Posters from current graduate students, demos from frontier-research projects, recruiters lurking politely. Underrated for sourcing talent that hasn't entered the founder pipeline yet.

Lobby

The rhythm of the city. Show up to one of these every week and by year-end you'll have a network you didn't pay for.

WeeklyFri · 8 am

LOOMUS · Builders Breakfast

San Francisco · Every Friday · Free Co-founder

Hosted weekly by Sophie since early 2025. Forty-two editions, 1,200+ founders attended. Coffee, no agenda, one rule: no slide decks. The most consistent weekly room in the city. RSVP via Luma.

WeeklyWednesdays

SF Founders Run & Coffee

Embarcadero · 7:00 AM Connection

Three miles along the bay, then coffee. Conversations get honest after the second mile.

BiweeklyMondays

SF AI · Infra Sellers Meetup

SoMa · Free · RSVP Customer

For people selling infrastructure into the AI stack — observability, evals, vector DBs, model APIs. Specific, technical, surprisingly social.

WeeklyThursdays

AI Collective · Build Night

SF Mission · Free Hire

One of the largest open-source AI communities in SF. Weekly build nights with rotating themes — RAG one week, agents the next, multimodal after that.

Monthly2025–26 LOOMUS host

LOOMUS · Deep Reading Club

SF + Online · Monthly · Open Paper

Twelve books read together since 2024, 340 readers across the cohort. The 2026 list leans heavily on AI, cognition, and the philosophy of machine learning. The slowest, deepest room in the city. Join the Luma channel.

Monthly2026

Generative AI SF · Meetup

Rotating venues · Free · 500+ attendees Connection

The largest public AI meetup in the city by attendance. Less intimate than AI Tinkerers, broader scope. Useful for hiring and for taking the room's temperature.
