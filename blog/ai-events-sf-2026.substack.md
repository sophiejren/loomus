# The Bay Area Room Map — 30 AI events, sorted by what you actually leave with

*Issue 01 · The Bay Digest · A weekly letter from LOOMUS · May 30, 2026*

---

San Francisco has too many AI events, and every roundup post is the same: *50 things to do, ranked by date*. This isn't one of those. Below is a 2D map of every room worth being in, plotted by intimacy and cadence — and then a matrix that answers the only question that matters: **what do I leave with?**

📅 **A new issue lands every Saturday morning** — same lens, fresh rooms.

LOOMUS curates this list every Saturday. Submissions: events@loomus.ai. The live calendar: [loomus.ai/events](https://loomus.ai/events).

## § 01 · The Room Map

*Every dot is one event. Vertical axis = intimacy (how many people in the room). Horizontal axis = cadence (how often it happens). Five archetype zones color-coded.*

> 📌 **Upload the Room Map image here** — file: `og-bay-digest-01.png` from /blog/ (1200×630). Substack supports drag-and-drop for images.

**The 5 archetypes:**

- ⬛ **Marquee Hall** — 1000+ people · annual flagships
- 🟧 **Workshop** — 50–200 people · recurring builds
- 🟨 **Salon** — 20–50 people · monthly dinners
- 🟩 **Lab** — 10–30 people · weekly research
- ⚪ **Lobby** — drop-in · casual

**Reading the map**: the clusters tell the story. Weekly intimate rooms dominate the lower-left — labs and lobbies. Annual flagship halls stack the upper-right. The interesting empty space is the middle: *mid-sized monthly events with curatorial seriousness* — the gap LOOMUS spends most of its programming budget on.

**Reading the matrix**: halls are best for customer + connection, but worst for finding a co-founder. **Salons are the only room that produces co-founders consistently.** Workshops are the only room where you'll meet someone willing to ship a prototype with you on Saturday. Labs are where the next year's papers get drafted. Lobbies are how you stay in the city's rhythm without burning a week. Stack one of each into your month and the math works out.

> *The right room beats the right idea. The right matrix beats the right room.*

## ⬛ Archetype 01 · Marquee Hall (1000+ · annual)

The flagship gatherings — the ones with multi-track programming and announcements that move markets. Worth the price of admission for the hallway alone, not the keynote.

**🗓 Jun 15–18, 2026 — Databricks Data + AI Summit** · Moscone, SF · $1,395–$1,895 · *4 days* · → Customer
The single largest data and ML conference of the year. Heavy on data engineering, governance, and the shift from analytics to agentic workflows.

**🗓 Jun 29 – Jul 2, 2026 — AI Engineer World's Fair** · Marines' Memorial, SF · $999–$1,699 · → Hire
The world's largest technical conference dedicated to building, deploying, and scaling AI systems.

**🗓 Sep 29 – Oct 1, 2026 — The AI Conference 2026** · Pier 48, SF · Single-track · → Idea
Single-track, founder-heavy gathering with views the rest of the conferences can't compete with.

**🗓 Nov 12, 2026 — Cerebral Valley AI Summit** · SF · Invite-only · → Connection

**🗓 Fall 2026 — AGI Summit & GenAI Summit 2026** · San Francisco · *LOOMUS flagship* · → Connection
Successor to GenAI Week SV and GenAI Summit Paris.

**🗓 Jan 2027 — Silicon Valley Robot Marathon · SVRM/27** · Bay Area Peninsula · *LOOMUS inaugural* · → Idea
