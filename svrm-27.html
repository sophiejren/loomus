07 Questions

Common questions.

What partners, teams and observers most often ask. Updated as the project evolves.

Q / 01Why a half-marathon, and why now?+

Beijing's 2025–26 E-Town events showed the format works — and the West needs its own open benchmark. A half-marathon is the cleanest public test we know: it measures perception, planning, energy and resilience in a single number — time-to-finish.

Q / 02What partner activations are possible?+

Beyond logo presence: Demo Zone exhibits, tech talks, custom merch, fueling stations, livestream segments, co-published whitepapers, post-race documentary co-production. Each partner receives a dedicated AE to design a bespoke activation.

Q / 03What safety standards apply to entered machines?+

Every machine must pass: dual-redundant E-stop, autonomous fall-recovery or safe-stop, SOTIF pedestrian avoidance, UL2271 battery fire safety, and event liability insurance. Full rulebook published Q4 2026.

Q / 04Is the event open to the public?+

Yes. Public viewing zones span the course, with a festival at the finish zone (Demo Zone, Speaker Stage, family experience). VIP access flows through partnership.

Q / 05Can international teams enter?+

Yes — we operate an International Teams Fund (partner-backed) to subsidize shipping for selected non-US teams, and offer visa and customs support.
